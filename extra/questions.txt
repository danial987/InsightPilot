Here is a list of dataset-related questions you can add to your `Questions.txt` file for the chatbot:

### General Dataset Questions:
1. What is the dataset summary?
2. How many rows are in the dataset?
3. How many columns are in the dataset?
4. What are the numerical features?
5. What are the categorical features?
6. What is the correlation matrix?
7. What is the memory usage of the dataset?
8. What is the average record size in memory?
9. Show the first rows of the dataset.
10. Show the last rows of the dataset.
11. Show the middle rows of the dataset.

### Numerical Features:
12. Show the numerical analysis of the dataset.
13. What are the most frequent values in the numerical features?
14. Show the distribution of numerical features.
15. Show histograms for numerical features.
16. Show skewness for numerical features.
17. Show kurtosis for numerical features.
18. What are the basic statistics for numerical features?

### Categorical Features:
19. Show the categorical analysis of the dataset.
20. What are the most frequent categories in [column_name]?
21. Show the top 5 categories in [column_name].
22. What is the distribution of categorical features?
23. How many unique values are in [column_name]?

### Data Quality and Warnings:
24. How many missing values are in the dataset?
25. How many duplicate rows are in the dataset?
26. Which features have missing values?
27. Which features have duplicate rows?
28. Are there any warnings in the dataset?
29. Which features have high cardinality?
30. Which features are highly skewed?

### Dataset Structure and Description:
31. What are the data types of the features?
32. Describe the dataset.
33. Show the variable types in the dataset.
34. Show the feature names.
35. What is the most frequent value in [column_name]?

These questions should provide a comprehensive set of inquiries that users can ask about their dataset. Let me know if you'd like to expand or customize this list further!
